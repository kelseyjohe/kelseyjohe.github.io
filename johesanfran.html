San Francisco: the Best and the Worst of the Best

My favorite neighborhood is North Beach

I LOVE North Beach because:

  • The weather is almost always sunny
  • There are lots of yummy gelato shops
  • The area is pretty quiet but has a lot of character
  • There's even a beach!
  • City Lights Bookstore is there!

I don't have a least favorite district so I'm going to name another one of my favorites: The Mission.

I LOVE The Mission because:

  • There's always something going on, whether it be farmer's market or a cultural event, the Mission is always buzzing.
  • There are lots of bookstores and ice cream spots in this neighnorhood as well!
  • The Roxie theatre is here.
  • All the good taquerias!
